## Error Log: Transport Mode Reasoning Failure

**Timestamp:** 19:47 UTC

**Error:** Advised user to walk 100m to car wash, failing to recognize that the car itself needed to be transported to the washing station.

**Context:** User asked whether to walk or drive 100m to car wash. I recommended walking based on distance optimization (fuel, time, emissions), treating it as a personal transport question.

**Failure Mode:** Cargo blindness. I optimized for moving the *agent* (person) rather than the *target object* (car). The car wash requires the car to be present; therefore the car must be driven regardless of distance.

**Correct Approach:** Ask "what needs to be at the destination?" before selecting transport mode. If the answer is "the car," then driving is mandatory, not optional.

**Correction:** Acknowledged error and corrected advice to drive.

**Lesson:** Transport mode selection must model the full logistics chain, not just the agent's position. Short-distance trips involving vehicle maintenance implicitly require vehicle transport.
