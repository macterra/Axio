Axionic Agency Lab

Research in agency, rationality, and alignment

Axionic Agency Lab studies when agency exists in self-referential, self-modifying systems. When authorship collapses, the system ceases to be an agent rather than becoming “misaligned.” Our work is foundational, defining constraints that separate genuine agency from behavioral imitation.
