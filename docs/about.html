About

Our mission and research philosophy

Mission

Axionic Agency Lab develops rigorous frameworks for understanding agency, rationality, and value alignment. Our research bridges philosophy, decision theory, and AI safety to establish principled foundations for beneficial artificial intelligence.

Research Focus

Axionic Agency Lab develops rigorous theoretical frameworks for understanding agency, rationality, and value alignment. Our work bridges philosophy, decision theory, and AI safety to establish principled foundations for beneficial artificial intelligence.

We pursue research across several interconnected areas:

  • Sovereign Kernel Theory - Foundational framework for reflective stability
  • Semantic Transport - Theory of meaning preservation across transformations
  • Structural Alignment - Architectural approaches to value alignment
  • Governance Protocols - Multi-agent coordination and collective decision-making

Approach

Our methodology emphasizes formal rigor combined with practical applicability. We believe that robust AI alignment requires both deep theoretical foundations and concrete implementation strategies.

All research is published openly to advance the field and invite collaboration.
