**Thesis.** Evil arises upstream of law, and eliminating it would require preemptive coercion that is itself harm — any system promising to eradicate evil must redefine it as disobedience or rule by suspicion. Culture is the containment layer; incarceration is defensive isolation, not punishment; the persistence of evil is the price of preserving authorship.
