**Thesis.** Benatar's asymmetry is rigged accounting resting on smuggled hedonism; embryo selection is not killing because possibility is not personhood — and there is no morally neutral stance once knowledge exists; accepting death is not virtue but complacency. Life is a wager on meaning-making, and agency favors placing it.
