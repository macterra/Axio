**Thesis.** The strongest rivals fail the same way: they locate objectivity in value content when it lives in structure. Parfit's Triple Theory mistakes coherence for ontology and begs the question toward realism; Rand's Objectivism correctly diagnoses sacrificial moralities but conflates instrumental success with ethical authority. Objectivity is a property of procedures, not propositions.
