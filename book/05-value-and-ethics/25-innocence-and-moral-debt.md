**Thesis.** Agency is a networked quantity: constraining another's viable futures creates a debt, and unpaid deficits propagate until the trust substrate collapses. Under coercive entanglement — human shields, embedded combatants — innocence is a defeasible constraint, not a veto: justified collateral harm remains possible under strict necessity, but always incurs moral debt that cannot be erased, only serviced.
