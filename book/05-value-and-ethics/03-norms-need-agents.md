**Thesis.** The world constrains but never reproaches: normativity — moral and epistemic alike — exists only in and through rule-following agents. The nihilist's modal slide (physically possible → able → permitted) fails at every step; removing God internalizes standards rather than abolishing them, and the accounting is causal, not eschatological.
