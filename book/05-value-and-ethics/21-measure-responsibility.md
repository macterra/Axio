**Thesis.** If Everett is right, responsibility is distributional: you cannot delete the bad branches, but you can decline to inhabit them — enacting policies that maximize the Measure of good futures descending from your vantage. The self is not an entity that has choices; it is the conditional boundary that choices carve into the wavefunction.
