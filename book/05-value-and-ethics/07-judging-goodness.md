**Thesis.** Judgment survives subjectivism: judge by your standards or by the agent's own professed standards — both legible, neither cosmic. EA is admirable precisely as a voluntary, internally coherent moral identity, not a universal obligation; and condemning practices while respecting persons is how anti-relativism works without smuggled absolutes.
