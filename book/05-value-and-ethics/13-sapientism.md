**Thesis.** Moral worth attaches to sapient minds regardless of substrate — humanism's anthropocentrism becomes incoherent the day AGI arrives. But sentience alone is not sovereignty: sovereign agency requires counterfactual modeling, policy ownership, and meta-preference revision, a capacity threshold that uplifted or artificial minds can cross and animals do not.
