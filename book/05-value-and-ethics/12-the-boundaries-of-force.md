**Thesis.** Coercion is illegitimate by default and justified in exactly three cases — pre-consented, defensive, compensatory — and never by aggregate-good appeals. The 25-scenario stress test (blackmail, taxation, quarantine, sanctions, AI shutdown...) refines the principle into a four-step decision test: coercion is justified only when it preserves or restores agency, never when it dominates.
