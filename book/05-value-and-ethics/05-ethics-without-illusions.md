**Thesis.** Consequentialism, deontology, and virtue ethics all survive the death of objective value once de-objectified: agent-relative decision theory, voluntarily chosen codes, and personal moral style. Every ethical system is a constructed framework you may adopt, blend, or reject — the LARP that knows itself.
