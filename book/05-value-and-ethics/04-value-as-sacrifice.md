**Thesis.** What an agent truly values is revealed only by what they sacrifice — willed AND within their power; hypothetical sacrifice is cheap talk. Opportunity cost is the yardstick of sincerity, and the free-rider 'proof' of objective public-goods value confuses coordination failure with value itself.
