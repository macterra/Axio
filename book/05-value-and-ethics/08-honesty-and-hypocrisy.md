**Thesis.** Honesty needs no divine enforcement — it follows from reality-alignment, reputation, and authenticity. Its inverse deserves taxonomy: eight species of linguistic moral hypocrisy, each lying in the register of virtue, each worse than plain lying because it corrodes trust and truth together.
