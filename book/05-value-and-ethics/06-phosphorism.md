**Thesis.** Vitalism inherits values from evolution; Valorism demands they be consciously chosen even at survival's expense; Phosphorism is the dialectical synthesis — an explicit, avowedly subjective, revisable list of chosen preferences: life over death, intelligence over ignorance, consent over coercion. Every hierarchical value system has an apex; choose yours deliberately.
