**Thesis.** IQ is a legitimate game-relative scalar: it measures effectiveness within a real family of abstract-rule games, anchored to shared variance, predictive within its domain — and controversy arises only when scores escape their game or get conflated with moral worth. What the profiles of Tao, Wolfram, and Deutsch add: greatness is talent compounded by discipline, courage, and communication.
