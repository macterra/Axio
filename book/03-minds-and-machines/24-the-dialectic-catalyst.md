**Thesis.** The practice that produced this book: AI as structured intellectual sparring partner — clarify, critique, synthesize. The naming matters: 'agent' wrongly implies autonomy, so 'catalyst' — an accelerant of human reasoning that possesses no will of its own. Persistent memory upgrades the catalyst from session-tool to cumulative cognitive partner, with the risks named early: dependency, drift, overfitting.
