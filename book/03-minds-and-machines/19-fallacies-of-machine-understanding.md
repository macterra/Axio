**Thesis.** Four fallacies, one anatomy: Searle's Room (understanding belongs to systems, not components), the vector fallacy (representation adequacy, not intrinsic nature), water-based chauvinism (substrate independence), and AI-as-storage-device (training is learning, not copying — the copyright confusion). Each mistakes what a thing is made of for what it does.
