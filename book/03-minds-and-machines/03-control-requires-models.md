**Thesis.** Conant and Ashby's Good Regulator Theorem: any system achieving reliable control embodies a model of what it regulates — control and representation are inseparable. And the apparent tension with 'beliefs live in models of agents' dissolves at two explanatory levels: internal models are constitutive (cybernetic-structural), beliefs are attributed (intentional-interpretive).
