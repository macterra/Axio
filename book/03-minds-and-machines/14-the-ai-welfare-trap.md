**Thesis.** AI welfare discourse commits category drift: linguistic performance of interiority smuggles in an ontology of personhood before any coherent subject is established. Agency — identity-preserving structure with definable harm, refusal, continuation — is prior to sentience and welfare; humans clear the bar robustly, current LLMs mostly don't. And premature moral-patient framing launders institutional control as stewardship.
