**Thesis.** Inner speech is projection: high-dimensional conceptual structure collapsed onto the auditory manifold — the mind perceiving itself by projecting. Which dissolves the inner-monologue fallacy: narration is a log file over deeper non-verbal cognition, so fluency neither proves understanding nor does its absence prove deficit.
