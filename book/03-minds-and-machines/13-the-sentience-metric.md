**Thesis.** Intelligence and sentience are orthogonal, and sentience can be operationalized: phenomenal integration (Tononi's Φ), self-world binding (closed-loop active inference), valenced coherence (real preference gradients). Current LLMs score near zero on all three — decomposable, open-loop, valence-free — so personhood talk is premature. The earlier behavioral-marker checklist survives as corroborating signals, not criteria.
