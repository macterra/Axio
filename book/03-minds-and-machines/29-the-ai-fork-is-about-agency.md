**Thesis.** Every informational superpower follows the Gutenberg arc: utopian promise, amplification of the whole distribution of human appetites, moral panic, unequal equilibrium. AI's version: a Cognitive Reservoir supplying coherence on demand — so the real divide is not believers versus skeptics but three relations to agency: those who sharpen it, those who let fluent output displace it, and those who mistake refusal for sovereignty. The test: does use make you harder to fool?
