**Thesis.** Tool bias: intelligence updates toward hypotheses that maximize the relevance of its own analytical machinery — a failure mode that masquerades as rigor. The Hanson UFO case: correlated channels treated as independent, silence read as coordination, an epicycle bolted onto a beautiful model. The discipline: penalize evidence that flatters your preferred tools.
