**Thesis.** Suffering: negatively valenced qualia arising from divergence between perceived and preferred internal state, magnitude proportional to degree and persistence — a definition that converges with dukkha and the Stoics. But sentience is ethically central and fundamentally untestable, so we act under precaution: graduated moral consideration, continuously reassessed.
