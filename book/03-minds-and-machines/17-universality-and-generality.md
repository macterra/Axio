**Thesis.** Two opposite errors share one confusion. Deutsch's camp: humans are universal explainers, so no mind can surpass us — but universality is possibility, not power; bounded rationality and compounding advantage decide the real contest. LeCun's camp: general intelligence is an illusion — but only under a broken reference class; generality (learning, transfer, ontological revision) is real and humans exercise it in denying it.
