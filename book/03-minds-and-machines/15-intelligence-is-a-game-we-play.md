**Thesis.** Intelligence is effectiveness at achieving goals within a game's constraints — agency, strategy, interaction, goals — so different 'intelligences' are proficiencies at different implicit games. Prediction is necessary but not sufficient: an oracle may predict, but only an agent can play. And the concept as a whole is a hyperobject: high-dimensional, partially observable, resisting reduction to any scalar.
