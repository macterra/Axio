**Thesis.** Meaning began with the first genuine symbol — Peirce's triad of sign, object, interpretant — which makes meaning coeval with life itself: RNA-world sequences interpreted by minimal agents. And somewhere in the hominin line, recursive imagination gave one mind temporal depth: the future began guiding behavior, inverting causality.
