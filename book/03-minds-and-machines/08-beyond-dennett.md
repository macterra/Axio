**Thesis.** Dennett demolished the Cartesian Theater and then stopped one subsystem short: his multiple-drafts narrator is the Controller, and its introspective blindness shows nothing about whether some other subsystem experiences. The Modeler is the missing experiencer — with privileged access, pre- and post-saccadic states, and non-linguistic content — explaining Dennett's data while locating what he couldn't find.
