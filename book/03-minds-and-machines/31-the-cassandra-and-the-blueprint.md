**Thesis.** Warnings against decisive technology function as blueprints: decades of AGI-doom argument made AGI thinkable and urgent — the fear of the race created the race. And a firsthand corrective: the author attended Yudkowsky's first public talk (~2001), when the answer was a global Sysop superintelligence; the early answer and the current ban share one root error — absolutism about a branching future.
