**Thesis.** A terminological ladder the whole part depends on: awareness (adaptive response) is necessary but insufficient for sentience (subjective experience), which doesn't require self-awareness; sentience and sapience are orthogonal in principle but evolutionarily entangled; and thinking versus feeling is a false divide — deliberative and affective cognition are both information processing.
