**Thesis.** A model is a structured representation preserving task-relevant distinctions, adequate always relative to a purpose. Implicit vs explicit models, generative capacity as the model/dataset boundary, compression as the enabler of generalization — and the Sphex wasp shows a lookup table is a real (if degenerate) model when the world is small and stable.
