**Thesis.** The Turing Test was a Bayesian threshold, not a definition — and LLMs have crossed it; continued disbelief is ontological displacement. The Loebner Prize died just before transformers would have trivialized it, exposing the deception premise. The successor: coherence under adversarial interrogation, on four axes — temporal, causal, goal, reflective — judged by reality, not by a human.
