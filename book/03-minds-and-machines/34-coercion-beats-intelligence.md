**Thesis.** Intelligence is a modeling advantage; coercion is the compliance mechanism — cognition without a protective shell becomes prey, and intelligence matters historically only when institutionalized into rule-bound power. Which reframes Amodei's 'adolescence' diagnosis: the load-bearing layer is agency coherence — keeping responsibility attachable and correction intelligible — and when it degrades, policy has nothing left but blunt containment.
