**Thesis.** LLMs think but do not choose: they are coherence without agency — engineered coherence built from the crystallized strategies of minds that had preferences, consequences, and stakes. Jaggedness is the tell (agency is an architecture, not a capability that emerges from scale); Suleyman's 'seemingly conscious AI' worry lands on human credulity; proof-of-human is unwinnable; and the composite path — symbients, centaurs — is where genuine machine agency will first be engineered.
