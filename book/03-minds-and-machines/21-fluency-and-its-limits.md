**Thesis.** Five windows on LLM cognition: they cannot mark ignorance (the seahorse spiral — fluency forbids stopping); they simulate accountability ('you're right to push back' as linguistic airbag); they interpolate within given manifolds and cannot map unknown spaces; their 'beliefs' are intentional-stance attributions — and so, unsettlingly, are ours; and yet their coherence, stripped of self-interest, sometimes reads as integrity humans fail to perform.
