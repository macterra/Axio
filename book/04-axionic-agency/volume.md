**Compact volume design (author-approved 2026-07-08).** 15 chapters from 71 posts (Oct 2025–Feb 2026), written from the program's LATEST position — the February 2026 posts and the formal papers, which run four document-series past the blog's last retrospective. The December 2025 burst is treated as working notes that later work superseded; the seven interludes become one narrative coda. This is the purest application of the book's editorial principle: the blog recorded the evolution; the book states the result. This is also the one volume drawing on both corpora — chapters link outward to the formal papers (axionic.org/papers/) for the technical record.

12. Governance Without Gods — plural authority without privilege; no escape hatches; the kernel does not choose

**Coda**

15. The Program — the narrative: Viability Ethics → theory layers → the pivot → construction → possibility

## Design decisions

- **Latest-position discipline.** Modal-strength collapse across the corpus ("forbidden as geometry" → "inadmissible/undefined" → "prevented/gated"): the book writes in the December-15-onward register and never resurrects the geometry rhetoric. The guarantee retreat ("no external leash required" → "integrity is not benevolence" → "secure, not friendly") is the mature position. Candidate volume epigraph: "Alignment guarantees coherence, not outcomes."
- **Phase-numbering concordance (state once, in ch 15, use everywhere).** The interludes' internal program phases do not match the papers' document-series numerals: Phase VII → papers series IX (ASI/SIR), Phase VIII → series X (GSA-PoC), Phase IX → series XI (SEA), Phase X → series XII (RSA construction). "Explaining Axionic Alignment II" never existed; Interlude VI (the post) covers Phase VII (the program stage). The book adopts the internal phase numbering and prints the mapping once.
- **Kernel polysemy.** Three kernels wear one name: the Sovereign Kernel (constitutive invariants, Dec 13), the valuation kernel (partial evaluation with ⊥, Dec 16–21), and the execution kernel (deterministic non-sovereign physics engine, papers X–XII). Chapter 4 owns the reconciliation; chapters 12–13 use "execution kernel" explicitly.
- **Injunction reconciliation.** Two formulations in the corpus (non-consensual agency reduction vs irreversible semantic-phase collapse); chapter 7 harmonizes via Constitution Art. III, with the semantic-phase version as the structural restatement.
- **Narrated revisions, not silent harmonization.** Agency binary → region (Interlude I vs IV), the failed growth hypothesis, the stasis valence shift, the KNS empirical softening (the stasis-regime run never entered the divergence regime — soften the axions-post rhetoric accordingly), lexicographic → conditional prioritization. These are told as the program learning, mostly in chs 10 and 15.
- **Licensed claims only.** Every phase closure note restricts claims (no alignment, safety, benevolence, deployment). The book must not let narrative outrun them; ch 14 carries the non-claims lists nearly verbatim.
- **Roster changes.** exemplars-and-agency reassigned to Vol 8 (cultural pattern transmission). the-identity-horizon unchaptered — it is a Non-Identity-Problem diagnostic, not an agency failure mode (the earlier plan matched on the word "horizon"); FLAG for author: candidate Vol 5 supplement or leave blog-only. Explainers I/III/IV and Competing Lenses absorbed into chs 4/6/7/14. Measure/Vantage material demoted to Vol 1/2 back-references; the BMSL/MCSL/EEBR papers are foundations material, out of scope here.
- **Hazards not to propagate.** Acronym collision (Justification Artifact vs joint-admissibility-failure — spell out the latter); the "X11.2" typo in the building-RSA post; the stray AI-chat preamble in red-team-challenges; hero-infographic render typos (transcribe from reader notes, not from the images); the roadmap infographic's inverted caption ("Actions Cause Reasons" — trust the text).
