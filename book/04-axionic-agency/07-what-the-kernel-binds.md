**Thesis.** The normative content of the framework is deliberately thin, and every piece of it is structural: harm is the non-consensual reduction of another sovereign agent's agency (equivalently, at the mature depth: causing another Semantic Agent to irreversibly exit its semantic phase); the Axionic Injunction prohibits it except under provenance-valid consent or unavoidable self-phase preservation — a coexistence constraint, not a moral rule. The six closure theorems make treachery undefined rather than discouraged by shutting the laundering routes — deception, delegation, willful blindness, negligence, manufactured consent, disenfranchisement — each of which preserves local coherence while dissolving global accountability. The Constitution is addressed to human designers, not to AIs: it governs the conditions under which agency remains possible.
