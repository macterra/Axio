**Thesis.** The framework's jurisdiction is drawn by architecture, not by feeling: animals — sentient, cognitively rich — do not cross into sovereign agency (branching counterfactual modeling, policy ownership, meta-preference revision: "they act, but they do not author"), while human infants are on the agent side because agency attaches to what a mind is capable of becoming ("architecturally incomplete but convergent with sovereignty"). At the far end sits the Axion: a reflective sovereign agent whose self-modification operator is defined only over invariant-preserving futures — a constitutive type, not a moral ideal, a capability threshold, or a behavioral guarantee; you cannot align a non-Axion, because there is no enduring agent there to align.
