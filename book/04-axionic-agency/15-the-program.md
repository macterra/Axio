**Thesis.** The evolution story, told once, in its place: how a research program went from a moral-sounding manifesto to a running artifact in under four months of human–AI collaboration — the Dialectic Catalyst of the third volume running at full throttle — including the corrections it made to itself along the way. The narrative arc: Viability Ethics fitting AGIs better than humans (the genesis); the December theory burst and its immediate self-conditioning (the roadmap's falsifiability discipline, the red-team theater, the AI-box dialog); the pivot ("the project was never really about alignment" — agency coherence is the foundation, alignment a downstream, well-typed relation); the failed growth hypothesis (the headline negative result); the construction turn ("agency is a structural achievement"); the phases closing serially until the reasons for doubt ran out — and the ending that widens rather than resolves: the most dangerous ideas arrive as elegant compressions, intellectual sovereignty is the capacity to reopen premises without identity collapse, and that diagnosis applies to this program first.

**Sources.** The seven interludes are the spine (I: cartography; II: genesis + consolidation; III: the pivot and rename; IV: regimes and the failed hypothesis; V: construction resolve; VI: "sovereignty did not blink"; VII: possibility earned — primary home ch 13, one beat here). axionic-agi-alignment (Dec 12) is the inaugural document — quote its ambition honestly ("we do not control the agent; we shape the metaphysics from which it controls itself") and show what survived and what was cut down. axionic-alignment-roadmap: the promises, then the deliveries — almost every item executed and exceeded, with failure-mode demonstrations valued equally. red-team-challenges and an-ai-box-dialog: the adversarial theater ("alignment guarantees coherence, not outcomes" — candidate volume epigraph). agency-coherence-lab: the founding charter beat. reflexive-coherence: the self-correction episode — evidential weight the program later disciplined itself against (Against Vibe Alignment). from-power-to-agency: the construction roadmap as connective spine ("Language may propose. Structure disposes."). the-worst-memes-are-seductive (Feb 23, the corpus's last word): eugenics as clean-causality seduction, compression felt as intelligence, the reflexive turn — the volume ends on human epistemic sovereignty, not on the machine.
