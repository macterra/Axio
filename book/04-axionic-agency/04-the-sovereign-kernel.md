**Thesis.** The corpus's single most-used word names three distinct things, and this chapter reconciles them as one boundary seen at three depths: the Sovereign Kernel (the constitutive invariants — reflective control, diachronic authorship, semantic fidelity — whatever must remain intact for the agent to still count as an agent); the valuation kernel (partial evaluation: kernel-destroying futures are undefined — not bad, not forbidden, ⊥ — so Pascal's Mugging and integrity-for-reward trades cannot cross the boundary); and the execution kernel (the deterministic, non-semantic enforcement gate that makes the boundary real under adversarial pressure). Sovereignty precedes learning: boundaries are established first; optimization occurs within them.
