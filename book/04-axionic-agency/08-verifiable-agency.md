**Thesis.** Alignment claims without causal provenance are not merely uncertain — they are non-falsifiable: behavioral observation cannot distinguish reasoning from retrieval, response from replay, constraint from simulated constraint ("in such a regime, 'alignment' becomes a compliment rather than a property"). The verification ladder built in five steps: causal traces committed in tamper-evident form and stress-tested by counterfactual mutation; minimal causal interfaces proving the kernel needs no semantic access; the discovered boundary — pure coherence can be fabricated by a careful pseudo-agent — closed by anchoring (kernel-generated unpredictable salt forcing real computation under adversarial uncertainty); and kernel integrity itself enforced as partiality, with every bypass attempt (delegation laundering, anchor reuse, semantic reframing, split-brain compliance) blocked at the actuation boundary. Trust requires skin in the game at the level of computation.
