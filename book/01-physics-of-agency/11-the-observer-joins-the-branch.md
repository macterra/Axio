**Thesis.** Observation is physical correlation: the observer joins an already-existing branch rather than creating reality. The cat and Wigner's friend dissolve; the Heisenberg cut evaporates (no royal observer); Occam's razor counts assumptions, not entities (the starlight analogy); and what quantum mechanics killed was classical object realism, never realism itself.
