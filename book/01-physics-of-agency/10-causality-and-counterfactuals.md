**Thesis.** Causation defined without mystery: a causes b iff every descendant timeline from their nearest common ancestor that contains a also contains b. The definition pays immediately: ideas rigorously move atoms (mental causation without spookiness), and counterfactuals are real branches with real Measure, so probabilities attach to them.
