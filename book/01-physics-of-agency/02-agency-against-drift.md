**Thesis.** Agency is a physical phenomenon on a par with energy, entropy, and information: an embedded agent with a predictive model spends energy to bias reality toward preferred futures, in opposition to drift — the statistical slide toward entropy. Every act of intentional control is thermodynamic work.
