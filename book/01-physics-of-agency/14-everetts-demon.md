**Thesis.** Laplace's demon foresaw one future; Everett's demon foresees a branching structure of genuinely realized outcomes. Quantum fluctuations in neural processing amplify into divergent macroscopic decisions — chess moves, conversations, hockey games — so everyday unpredictability is ontological, not epistemic: Candyland's outcome is fixed at the shuffle; a chess game is a branchcone.
