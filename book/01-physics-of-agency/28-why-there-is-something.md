**Thesis.** The Modal Realization Principle: all physically admissible states are realized on some timeline, and absolute nothingness is not an admissible state — it is a non-denoting null, expressible only as a counterfactual contrast inside an already-existing modal framework. The meta-question 'why does the modal framework exist?' is ill-typed: explanation applies only within a space of alternatives.
