**Thesis.** The QBU is a directed acyclic graph of quantum events whose paths are timelines — a static ensemble of block universes related by branch structure. Pattern Identifiers select timelines: Strong PIs (genotypes, connectomes) guarantee a common causal ancestor; Weak PIs (names) cut across unrelated histories.
