**Thesis.** Tiny differences do not occasionally matter — they inevitably compound: chaotic dynamics plus branching mean every micro-decision propagates (different crossing time, different people conceived, different civilizations). Klaas's Fluke is the empirical counterpart; Shermer's rubber-band model of history is the intuition that has to die.
