**Thesis.** The kybit quantifies intentional influence: the KL divergence between the outcome distribution without and with the agent's intervention. Control has a thermodynamic floor — each kybit costs at least kT ln 2 of work (the Law of Control Work), Landauer's principle extended from erasure to steering.
