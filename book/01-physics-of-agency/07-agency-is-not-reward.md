**Thesis.** Replacing reward-maximization with future-path occupancy is progress — reward is fuel, not essence — but occupancy is blind: parasites, coercive institutions, and power-seeking AIs maximize it too. Agency is the preservation and navigation of a coherent field of admissible futures, and admissibility is normative structure occupancy lacks.
