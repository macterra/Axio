**Thesis.** The Axio frameworks have an intellectual lineage: Popper and Bartley's fallibilism becomes Conditionalism; Everett's collapse-free branching becomes the QBU's mechanics of agency; Deutsch's synthesis — quantum computation, constructor theory, hard-to-vary explanations — becomes constructors as agency-builders. The volume opens by placing itself in that line and saying where it goes further.
