**Thesis.** The volume's premier worked example: Freud persists not as science but as memetic architecture. His achievement was architectural rather than empirical — he seeded language with replicators that rewired Western explanation toward hidden causes, demoted testimony, and licensed interpretive authority over the agent; once embedded, the memes no longer required psychoanalysis to survive (persistence through substitution: libido→neurotransmitters, complexes→schemas). Each meme is a compressed causal accusation asserting depth, hidden motive, and interpretive authority in one word. The moral reorientation followed: demoted intention diffuses responsibility, supervision acquires a benevolent sheen, therapeutic governance and the secular confessional become ordinary. Freud stuck because he solved modernity's coordination problem — determinism without despair, meaning without theology, critique without revolution — and the agency cost is the frame this book exists to reverse: the agent modeled as fragmented, opaque, interpretively subordinate.

**Sources.** echoes-of-freud (Dec 2025), standalone. The four-tier meme inventory is the centerpiece — preserve as boxed lists: (1) directly Freudian (unconscious, repression, projection, rationalization, denial, ego, narcissism…); (2) ecosystem (inferiority complex, defense mechanisms, mommy/daddy issues); (3) rewired pre-existing terms (trauma, complex, fantasy, symbol); (4) functionally Freudian folk diagnostics (compensating, power trip, triggered, unresolved issues).
