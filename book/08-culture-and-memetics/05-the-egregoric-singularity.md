**Thesis.** The Singularity was not a future AI event: it was the historical fusion of memes and human minds into egregores — collective thought-forms with agency-like properties (self-preserving, reproductive, adaptive) for which humans are the substrate. Writing, religion, law, and markets are memetic life-forms that recruited us; AI is the midwife of memetic reproduction, not the origin. Plato's Cave, reconceived: the shadows are alive, there is no exit because we are part of the projector, and every ideology of liberation is a passage into another cavern — so freedom is redefined as curation, distinguishing parasitic egregores (which siphon vitality into obedience) from symbiotic ones (which scaffold coherence without erasing autonomy). Nationalism is the worked example: a secular religion complete with liturgy, martyrs, sacred spaces, priests, and eschatology. And the dynamics run at every scale — a private body of thought past critical mass becomes a constructor, coherence compounding until invention yields to recombination.
