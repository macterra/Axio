**Thesis.** Three case studies of one mechanism, spined by psychiatry's five-symptom capture diagnostic: selective redefinition of terms; policy preceding evidence; asymmetric application of diagnostic principles; a chilling effect on inquiry; and institutional gatekeeping by activists. Psychiatry: diagnostic integrity subordinated to political narrative — "if psychiatry won't tell the truth about the mind, who will?" The academy: neutrality was the social contract and the only protection; declaring every field complicit forfeited the impartiality claim, and the 2025 funding retaliation followed — "by insisting that neutrality is complicity, they destroyed the neutrality that protected them" (capture invites counter-capture; power never flows one direction for long). Engineering: "decolonizing" curricula commit a category error — treating a universal predictive method as a culturally situated worldview — confusing verification (correspondence to physical outcomes) with meaning (evaluation within cultural context), displacing competence with reflection; "an engineer who reflects well but designs poorly is not an engineer," and "when truth becomes negotiable, safety becomes negotiable."
