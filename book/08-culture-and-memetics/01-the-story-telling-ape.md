**Thesis.** Humans are Homo Narrans: gene–meme coevolution made narrative the native format of cognition, identity, and coordination — stories are the highest-fitness meme vehicles because emotional and moral resonance beat accuracy, and humans consistently prefer psychological coherence over empirical truth. The consequence plays out in history itself: legend systematically colonizes fact. Arthur, Socrates, and Jesus form a spectrum from feeble to secure historical motte, each overwhelmed by an expansive cultural bailey, because "human imagination is ravenous — it does not tolerate bare kernels of fact."
