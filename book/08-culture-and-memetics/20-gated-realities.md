**Thesis.** Capture's final form is self-imposed: audiences capturing themselves. The progressive platform enclave is structurally identical to white flight — voluntary separation framed as necessity, moral inversion converting departure into righteousness, disgust avoidance, echo reinforcement: "people do not simply leave; they redefine leaving as liberation," and "yesterday's bigots fled cities to avoid difference; today's progressives flee platforms to avoid dissent. Both call it safety." The mechanism beneath is developmental: Kegan's Socialized Mind, for which disagreement is felt as existential threat, seeking relational-ideological safety and calling it harassment refuge — with the Self-Authoring Mind (welcoming intellectual conflict, decoupling self-worth from group alignment) as the alternative. The cost is an epistemic immune disorder: an over-reactive moral immune system attacking the culture's own error-correction capacity — gated realities, epistemic anemia, an archipelago of moral comfort withering from self-curated ignorance rather than censorship.
