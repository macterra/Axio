**Thesis.** Capture is structural, not partisan — the proof is the woke right: the fiercest anti-woke critics adopting woke methods point for point (grievance identity politics, narrative over facts, identity epistemology, purity spirals, speech policing, anti-liberal dogma). "Fighting wokeism by adopting its methods is akin to battling religious dogmatism by establishing an Inquisition"; the shared substrate is tribal psychology — liberal, rationalist norms are the unnatural achievement. But the symmetry claim must not itself become caricature: Lindsay's mirror-syllogisms of Left and Right are rhetorically sharp and analytically wrong, resting on the equivocation of moral equality with empirical sameness. The Left syllogism (unequal outcomes → unfair system) straw-mans the serious progressive position; the Right syllogism (unequal outcomes → unequal people) collapses descriptive variation into normative inequality. The real divide is whether inequality of results is sufficient evidence of systemic injustice — and "equal dignity under the law is compatible with unequal outcomes in life."
