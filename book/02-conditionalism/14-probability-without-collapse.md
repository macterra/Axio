**Thesis.** The Everettian probability problem dissolves once Measure and Credence are distinguished: the Regret/Typicality Lemma shows an agent whose credences diverge from branch measure is, weighted by measure, almost certainly outperformed — the Born rule as a normative prescription rather than an axiom.
