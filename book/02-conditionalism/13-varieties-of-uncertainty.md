**Thesis.** Timeline (indexical) uncertainty does not exhaust credence: logical, semantic, and metaphysical uncertainty are legitimate objects of probabilistic belief, and Garrabrant's Logical Induction shows how credences over mathematics can be coherent without an objective probability underneath.
