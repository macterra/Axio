**Thesis.** Faith is belief held immune to calibration — a frozen update rule — and it is never epistemically justifiable: every apparent defense (pragmatic, moral, existential) either reduces to calibrated confidence or admits self-sealing dogma. Choosing worldviews for beauty inverts inference the same way.
