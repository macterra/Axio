**Thesis.** Wisdom begins when an agent becomes answerable to constraint and explicit about frame: reality constrains, interpretation is conditional, agency is bounded, authority requires justification. And truth alone isn't enough — it must be transmitted under epistemic constraint, without surrendering to memetic fitness.
