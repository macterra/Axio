**Thesis.** Priors dominate perception until evidence forces an update — and the characteristic failure of intelligence is not ignorance but rationalization: any decision can be storied into coherence. Meta-rationality is testing the frame itself; irrationality is refusing to update under stable disconfirming feedback.
