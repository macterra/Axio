**Thesis.** Ask one diagnostic question of any organization: is it funded by voluntary exchange, or by payments extracted under credible threat of harm? The taxonomy — VFO (voluntarily funded), EFO (extortion funded), RFO (resource funded) — is deliberately neutral and deliberately symmetric: states and protection rackets share a funding mechanism, differing in institutionalization and acceptance, not method. The necessity argument runs backward: if state services were worth their cost, voluntary financing would emerge; the need for compulsion is itself evidence. And extraction need not be visible or even intentional — inflation plus nominal capital-gains taxation takes the same wealth twice through a structural blind spot, no villain required.
