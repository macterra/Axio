**Thesis.** Only individuals concretely exist as conscious, valuing agents; attributing mental states and moral priority to groups is a category error. The individualism/collectivism axis is orthogonal to left–right — the volume's real subject is that axis, and everything that follows (coercion, rights, speech, justice, governance) runs on the premise stated here: groups are useful abstractions; cooperation is instrumental, voluntary, and recoverable without subordination.

**Sources.** why-im-an-individualist (May 2025, the volume's earliest post): the five-criterion comparison and the orthogonality claim. The scorecard/listicle form should be rewritten as prose — content sound, register early.
