**Thesis.** The theoretical heart of Part III. Popper's paradox of tolerance dissolves under Conditionalism — tolerate all non-coercive speech regardless of offense; never tolerate coercive harm — but the mature boundary is the December 2025 causal ladder: classify utterances by causal-functional role, not sentiment. Tier 1 (expression, however hateful) is categorically protected — hate-speech laws are secular blasphemy laws, and "speech is violence" loads rifles. Tier 2 (epistemic violence: systematic dehumanization with demonstrated causal coupling to harm) is regulable only under a five-condition test — intentionality, causal engagement, targeting, credible pathway, non-consent — replacing Brandenburg's imminence with causal-pathway analysis. Tier 3 (incitement that delegates violence) is violence, not speech at all.
