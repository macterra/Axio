**Thesis.** Anarchy means absence of coercive monopoly, not absence of hierarchy or order. Micro: voluntary "benevolent dictatorships" — theme camps, companies, communities — are fully anarchic so long as exit is unpunished; leadership must be provisional, conditional, and chosen, and coercion begins where exit is forbidden. Macro: the international system already IS a functioning anarchy — no sovereign above the parties, no police, no monopoly of violence — yet treaties hold, conventions bind, and order arises from reciprocity and power balance ("coordination mistaken for sovereignty"). Statists already live in anarchy at the largest scale; the argument is over where the same architecture may be trusted next.
