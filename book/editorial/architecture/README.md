# Phase 2 Architecture Audit

This directory records the Phase 2 structural audit of *Axio*. The audit read the Preface, book Introduction, all nine volume introductions, and each volume's final chapter against the Phase 1 argument map and dependency controls.

## Documents

- `book-wide.md` confirms the revised nine-volume architecture and records cross-volume decisions.
- `01-physics-of-agency.md` through `09-meaning.md` record the structural verdict, dependency audit, pressure points, ending assessment, and recommended actions for each volume.

## Status

The architecture is **confirmed with targeted revision**:

- Retain nine volumes and the current order.
- Treat Volumes 1/2 and 4/5 as reciprocal hinges rather than a strict dependency ladder.
- Keep Volumes 3 and 7 unsplit, with part divisions made visible in navigation.
- Move “The Price of Breath” from Volume 6 to the end of Volume 7.
- Preserve the Volume 8 → 9 handoff and Volume 9's double envoi.
- Defer changes to manuscript prose and framing until the relevant volume edit is approved.

These files are editorial decisions, not published book material. No recommendation here changes the manuscript until it is implemented in a later dedicated PR.
