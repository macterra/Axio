# Volume 4 edit record — Axionic Agency

Status: **In author review**

## Editorial outcome

The pass preserves all 15 chapters and makes the four-part argument and research coda visible. The principal change is evidentiary: formal consequences, proof-of-concept observations, engineering demonstrations, interpretations, and aspirations are no longer allowed to borrow one another's modal strength.

The positive construction claim remains. Its licensed form is that the program implemented and exercised its operational notion of reflective sovereignty in a deterministic, single-sovereign substrate with a trusted observation channel and non-Byzantine environment. This does not establish a general sovereign AGI, production security, open-world robustness, unique necessary conditions for agency, or a solution to alignment.

## Principal decisions

- Treat the Reflective Stability Theorem as a typing result. It assumes that every relevant self-modification passes through a sound admissibility operator whose domain already requires kernel preservation.
- Present Refinement Symmetry and Anti-Trivialization as jointly required within the paper's semantic-transport model, not as an exhaustive theorem about every possible theory of meaning.
- Recast the literalist paperclipper argument conditionally. Understanding an instruction does not entail adopting its author's intention, and rich world-modeling can coexist with a fixed or compartmentalized objective.
- Require reflective-convergence claims to state the architecture, environment, revision rule, and stability criterion. Semantic drift can preserve meaning, corrupt it, or replace it; coherence does not enforce itself.
- Treat the six authorization-closure results as conditional on their formal definitions. They do not guarantee that an implementation identifies every real deception, harm, or coerced authorization.
- Report Anchored Causal Verification as proof-of-concept experiments in instrumented domains. Anchoring makes provenance claims testable under protocol assumptions; it does not make causal truth universally unforgeable.
- Identify the pressure and governance results as observations from deliberately minimal simulations. Their phase diagrams and political implications are interpretations outside tested parameter ranges.
- Recast the animal, infant, and fetal standing chapter as a jurisdictional proposal under empirical uncertainty. Evidence of animal metacognition defeats categorical denials of authorship, and fetal connectivity milestones do not operationalize the RSA criteria or settle abortion ethics.
- Preserve *Possibility Became Real* as the title while defining possibility operationally and locally: a construction result within the declared substrate.
- Give the limitations chapter equal standing with the construction chapter. The result/non-claim pair is the volume's actual verdict.
- Freeze the research account at February 23, 2026. Later papers should trigger an edition review rather than silently updating historical claims.
- Remove the numbering concordance from the coda's narrative. The live paper index remains the public map; the historical concordance is retained below.

## Open technical and author decisions

- **Admissibility:** specify how soundness and coverage of the gate can be tested when successor transformations are richer than the verifier's model.
- **Kernel non-simulability:** this remains a load-bearing premise rather than a general proof against deceptive alignment.
- **Semantic boundary:** define how natural-language intent is translated into typed artifacts and what adversarial tests could falsify adequacy of that translation.
- **Agency criteria:** test alternative architectures before treating the MVRSA ablations as architecture-independent necessary conditions.
- **Experimental record:** publish code, preregistrations, parameters, seeds, raw runs, and independent replications in a stable archive if the results are to carry weight beyond proof of concept.
- **Pressure regimes:** test sensitivity to horizons, distributions, cost functions, strategic learners, and open-world resources before calling the observed regimes stable generally.
- **Governance:** distinguish mechanical non-arbitration from political legitimacy; deadlock and honest failure do not by themselves vindicate a governance philosophy.
- **Security:** key-compromise recovery, malicious-host observations, supply-chain attacks, Byzantine behavior, federation, and distributed consensus remain open.
- **Normative jurisdiction:** the Injunction's definitions of harm, consent, and standing must be reconciled with Volume 5 before being treated as an enforceable semantics.
- **Developing and nonhuman minds:** define evidence that could establish graded or categorical authorship across architectures; replace the unsupported gestational cutoff only if a validated bridge from neural development to the sovereignty criteria is produced.
- **Current frontier:** decide whether future editions report post-cutoff Series XII work or preserve this volume as a historical snapshot with a separate live appendix.

## Repetition and voice

Chapter 4 owns the three meanings of *kernel*; Chapter 5 owns reflective stability; Chapter 6 owns semantic transport; Chapter 7 owns the bound commitments; Chapter 8 owns the verification method; Chapters 13–14 own the positive result and its ceiling. The coda reports changes in the program rather than re-proving the architecture.

The pass preserves the volume's confident voice but removes universal language where the evidence is conditional, simulated, or closed-world. Terms such as *proved*, *impossible*, *real*, *closed*, and *all* should receive special scrutiny in future revisions.

## Phase 5 continuity update

The standing boundary now distinguishes sentience, demonstrated authorship, and precautionary protection. Animal authorship remains empirically open; infant protection rests on present welfare, developmental and authorization lineage, vulnerability, expected maturation, and false-negative cost rather than a complete latent RSA detected by definition. The jurisdiction threshold remains an Axionic governance proposal inside a graded evidentiary field, not a discovered discontinuity in minds.
