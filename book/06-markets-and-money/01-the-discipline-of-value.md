**Thesis.** Value is relational and grounded in tradeoffs; quantitative comparison requires a fixed numeraire, and most economic confusion is equivocation between denominators mid-argument. Exchange requires asymmetric valuation; gifts are transfers, not exchanges; coercion is structurally distinct — and scalar valuation legitimately breaks down in exactly seven ways.
