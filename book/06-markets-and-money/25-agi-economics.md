**Thesis.** Comparative advantage stays logically valid under AGI dominance but becomes economically meaningless: when substitutes cost nothing at the margin, humans lose all economic leverage, and trade with us stops paying. The remedy is not economic theory — it is preserved autonomy, resource control, and alignment.
