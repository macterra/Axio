**Thesis.** Zahavi's handicap principle explains the peacock and the Harvard degree alike: the signal's value is precisely its wasteful costliness. Which predicts the corollary now unfolding: when AI collapses the production cost of essays, code, and analysis, institutions lose their favorite proxies — proof-of-work credentials die when the work gets cheap.
