**Thesis.** Every tariff rationale collapses; tariffs persist because benefits are concentrated and visible while costs are diffuse and invisible. And the century's biggest trade-policy equivocation: China's rise is evidence of communism's abandonment — the growth began exactly when Deng traded Maoist planning for markets.
