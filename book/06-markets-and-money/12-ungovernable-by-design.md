**Thesis.** Any open, computationally expressive substrate — Bitcoin, DNA, language — cannot be regulated at the substrate level: consensus enforces structure, not intent, and syntactic limits are routed around by re-encoding semantics. And Bitcoin's energy is not waste: waste depends on value produced, mining monetizes stranded energy, and the network buys financial sovereignty.
