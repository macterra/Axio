**Thesis.** Money is the ticket; wealth is the coat — and nearly every macro fallacy is mistaking one for the other. The layer stack: value (relational usefulness), wealth (controlled valuable capacity), capital (wealth deployed in production — a role, not an object), money (four-function coordination technology), currency (implementation), credit (claims across time).
