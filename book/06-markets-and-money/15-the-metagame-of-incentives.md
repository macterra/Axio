**Thesis.** Behavior follows incentives, not formal rules: metagames rewrite lower games without touching their rulebooks. Misalignment — optimizing level L degrades coherence at L+1 — is the default state of nested systems: five patterns, three dynamics (cascade, lock-in, runaway), one diagnostic question set.
