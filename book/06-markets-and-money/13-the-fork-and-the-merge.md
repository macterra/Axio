**Thesis.** Ethereum breached credible neutrality twice: the DAO fork proved social consensus overrides protocol, and the Merge made the override structural — proof-of-stake couples authority to retained capital, which compounds endogenously. Proof-of-work centralizes too, but its costs are external and continuously re-earned: dominance stays contestable. You cannot decentralize capital.
