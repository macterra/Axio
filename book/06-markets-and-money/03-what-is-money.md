**Thesis.** Currency is literally the common denominator: the scalar unit that makes incompatible subjective valuations commensurable — a mathematical technology with no intrinsic value. MMT fails because it imbues that neutral medium with governmental authority, ignores its hidden conditions (trust, stability, productive capacity), and flunks Hayek's knowledge problem.
