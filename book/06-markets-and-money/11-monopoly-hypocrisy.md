**Thesis.** The entity prosecuting monopolies is itself the only absolute monopoly — taxation, law, violence — and the only one not resting on consent; corporate dominance is fragile because voluntary. The minimum wage is the same inversion at the labor market: coercion wearing compassion's mask, pricing the least productive out of work entirely.
