**Thesis.** Modern wealth is deployed capital, not vaulted cash — every invested dollar is someone's income. The right question about a trillionaire is what the wealth does: concentrated capital is civilizational leverage (agency concentrates into network hubs), and the moral axis was never inequality — it is poverty and coercion.
