**Thesis.** Ideas are non-rivalrous, so IP was always a legal fiction under strain — and digital copying is ending it over decades, not overnight. Which sharpens the payment problem: someone must fund the open web, every candidate (reader, state, creator, advertiser) distorts, and the defensible answer is constrained contextual advertising plus budgeted micropayments — the billboard, not the transponder.
