**Thesis.** The gender wage gap is primarily an equilibrium of biology-rooted asymmetry, not discrimination: gestation imposes a non-delegable volatility penalty, markets price continuity, households rationally specialize. Policy can redistribute the cost; it cannot destroy it — the Structural Asymmetry Principle.
