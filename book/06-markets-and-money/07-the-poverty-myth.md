**Thesis.** Poverty is nature's baseline — the thing needing no explanation; wealth is the achievement. Extreme poverty fell from over 90% (1820) to under 9%; 'late-stage capitalism' is cultural pessimism wearing an economic label; and healthcare lacks market variety only because market competition there is largely illegal.
