**Thesis.** A price is a convergence point between subjective valuations — it reveals only that buyer valued above and seller below; the magnitudes stay hidden. Everything valued is in principle priceable ('priceless' = an infinite reservation price), and a project no one funds voluntarily is, by definition, not valuable enough — the calculation problem in miniature.
