**Thesis.** Prosperity structurally suppresses fertility — opportunity costs, quality-quantity tradeoffs, children flipped from assets to liabilities — and digital modernity adds the social layer: children are the supreme anti-optionality commitment in a civilization optimized for optionality. Housing norms (mortgage before maternity) lock the threshold behind decades of debt. No coercive fix is acceptable; the honest question is which voluntary rearrangements could matter.
